Kinari is a learning platform made to support you in everyday and work situations. Its purpose is to help you feel more confident, communicate clearly, and understand your basic rights. Kinari is designed to be easy to use and easy to understand, so you can learn at your own pace, no matter your background or level of education. The courses on Kinari focus on three main areas: communication, rights, and confidence. The communication lessons help you practise speaking clearly, listening to others, and expressing your thoughts in a calm and respectful way. The rights lessons explain important personal and workplace rights in simple language, helping you recognise unfair treatment and understand what steps you can take. The confidence activities help you believe in yourself, trust your voice, and feel comfortable speaking up when something does not feel right. Together, these courses are designed to support you in building important life skills. By improving communication, understanding your rights, and growing in confidence, Kinari helps you feel safer, stronger, and more prepared for real-life situations at work and beyond.
